About Me

Welcome to my personal portfolio. My name is Berk Kasimcan, and I am a mechanical engineer and researcher focused on building human-centered technologies at the intersection of robotics, haptics, and healthcare. My work is driven by a simple goal: designing systems that are affordable, accessible, and useful for real people. My goal is to combine mechanical and biomedical engineering skills to help people either with engineering solutions, policy writing, or volunteering.

Beyond titles and degrees, I’m motivated by curiosity and hands-on problem solving. Whether I’m prototyping a new haptic device, digging into interdisciplinary research, or experimenting in the kitchen, I enjoy learning by doing. This website is a snapshot of my academic work, projects, and the interests that shape how I think and build.
